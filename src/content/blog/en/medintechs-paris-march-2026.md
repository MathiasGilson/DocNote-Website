DocNote took part in MedInTechs 2026, a major event dedicated to healthcare innovation held at the Parc Floral de Paris, as a partner of the Surgical Innovation Village.

![DocNote booth at MedInTechs 2026 in Paris](/images/medintechs_03.26_-_2.jpg)

Over two days, the team presented DocNote and exchanged with many clinicians, entrepreneurs and public stakeholders committed to concretely transforming the healthcare system.

A highlight of the event was the opportunity to introduce DocNote to former French ministers Valérie Pécresse and Olivier Véran. A special thanks to Muriel Benitah for the invitation and the introductions.

![The Surgical Innovation Village at MedInTechs 2026](/images/medintechs_03.26_-_3.jpg)

As the first AI tool designed to automate the full range of administrative tasks a physician faces at the hospital — from pre-hospital settings and emergencies to ward follow-up notes, discharge letters and consultation reports — DocNote drew strong interest throughout the show.
