DocNote is proud to announce that it has been selected for the Initial Startup Support program of FONGIT, one of Switzerland's leading technology incubators, based in Geneva.

DocNote is an assistant designed to help physicians reduce their administrative burden. From a recorded consultation, a dictation or follow-up notes, it automatically generates structured medical documents — consultation reports, operative reports and discharge letters.

Tested across the full spectrum of clinical settings — in hospitals, in ambulatory care and at the patient's side — the solution now covers a large share of the clinical variations of everyday medical practice, thanks to feedback from physicians across specialties.

We warmly thank FONGIT for this support and for its commitment to the Geneva startup ecosystem. This is only the beginning.
