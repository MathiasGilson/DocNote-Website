DocNote reports can now be integrated directly into the Mediway electronic health record, thanks to a new feature developed with Mediway.

Concretely, the consultation is recorded and automatically structured with DocNote, and in a single click the report is integrated into a Mediway consultation, with no additional manipulation.

Designed to fit into Mediway users' existing workflow without changing their daily habits, the feature has a clear goal: save physicians time and make medical documentation more reliable.

A big thank you to Mediway for developing this feature. Physicians using Mediway can discover and try DocNote directly on our website.
