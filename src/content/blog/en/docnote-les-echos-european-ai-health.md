DocNote has been featured in Les Echos, one of France's leading business newspapers, in an in-depth analysis of the fast-growing market of AI assistants in healthcare.

In her article, journalist Marion Simon-Rainaud mapped the main European players reshaping the sector and positioned DocNote among the key names in the "medical scribing and document generation" segment.

Being cited alongside the leading actors of this rapidly evolving field is a meaningful recognition of the work accomplished by the entire DocNote team.

Beyond the competition driving the sector, the article reflects a strong conviction: AI has the potential to give clinical time back to caregivers by reducing the administrative burden they face every day.
