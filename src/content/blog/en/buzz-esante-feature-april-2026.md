Digital health media Buzz E-santé published an article on DocNote, highlighting a deliberately focused approach: being the first AI assistant fully dedicated to hospital workflows.

As the article notes, medical time is a scarce resource in hospitals. Between consultations, ward rounds, multidisciplinary meetings and unplanned emergencies, clinicians must also produce, complete, correct and sign a considerable volume of documents — consultation reports, discharge letters, ward notes, prescriptions and summaries.

Often experienced as an administrative weight that eats into time spent at the patient's bedside, this documentation is precisely the friction point DocNote targets, automating it across the full range of hospital tasks.

We warmly thank Rémy Teston and Buzz E-santé for this insightful piece on DocNote.
