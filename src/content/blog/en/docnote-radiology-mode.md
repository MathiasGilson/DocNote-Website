DocNote is launching its Radiology mode, a new model designed specifically for imaging workflows.

In real-world testing, a structured knee MRI report could be completed in under 90 seconds. The mode brings automatic structuring aligned with radiological standards, a shorter turnaround between examinations, less end-of-day reporting workload and simple integration into the existing environment.

The goal is not speed for its own sake. By reducing reporting time, the mode frees up time for what matters most: image analysis and clinical decision-making.
